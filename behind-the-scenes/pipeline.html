How It Works

Every joke gets a seed ID and passes through 8 stages. Each stage is owned by one or two team members. If a joke stalls, everyone knows where it's stuck.

1. Scouting

Zoe Sparks scours the internet for raw jokes, tags them by theme and risk, and drops them into the intake queue.

2. Writing

Milo Quip cleans up the joke and scripts it for short-form delivery — setup, punchline, timing, captions.

3. Casting

Riley Cast matches characters to roles based on the script, selecting personas and performance styles.

4. Visual Dev

Iris Doodle produces 2-3 concept options with storyboards, scene timelines, and master video prompts.

5. Greenlight

Jerry Gold picks the winning concept, writes a locked production brief, and sends it to the floor.

6. Production

Vince Clip generates voice-over, video scenes, captions, and assembles the final 9:16 short.

7. QA

Lena Cut polishes and Noah Check reviews. If it doesn't pass the rubric, it goes back.

8. Publish

Tasha Trend posts to YouTube, updates the site, and writes the studio article.
